Anne With An E

Anne with An E is a show about good morals. Treating others how you would want to be treated. The show is about an orphane girl named Anne that wanted to change how the world percieved those who were looked down upon, She has a golden heart and wishes to change the stereotypes of society. 'Anne with An E' is about accepting those who feel out of place in the community and to welcome them wholeheartedly.
